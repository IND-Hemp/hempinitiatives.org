Hi there. We’re HEMI®—a non-profit lead by a Board made up of farmers, manufacturing professionals, academics, scientists and government representatives.

HEMI stands for “Hemp Education & Marketing Initiatives”. Our vision is for agricultural hemp to be a widely embrased bio-based material and a favorite source of high-quality nutrition in the United States, trusted by consumers for its health benefits, reliability, and environmental sustainability.

So: what is The Goodness of Hemp?

OUR MISSION

Accelerate demand growth through strategic leadership and heightened market awareness of agricultural hemp products and solutions, while fostering new business opportunities through innovation, best practices, and partnerships that benefit U.S. farmers and processors.
